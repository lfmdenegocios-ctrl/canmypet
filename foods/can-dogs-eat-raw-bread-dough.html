Can dogs eat raw bread dough?

No — dogs should never eat raw bread dough.

Raw yeast dough expands in the warm stomach and ferments into alcohol, causing bloat and alcohol poisoning.

Why this verdict for dogs

Raw yeast dough expands in the warm stomach and ferments into alcohol, causing bloat and alcohol poisoning.

Warning signs to watch for

  • Swollen, bloated abdomen
  • Unproductive vomiting
  • Disorientation

⚠ If your dog ate raw bread dough

Act immediately — don't wait for symptoms.

  • Call your vet or a pet poison helpline right away.
  • Note how much was eaten and when.
  • Do not induce vomiting unless a professional tells you to.
