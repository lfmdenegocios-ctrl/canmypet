Can dogs eat pumpkin seeds?

Yes — dogs can safely eat pumpkin seeds.

Plain, unsalted pumpkin seeds are non-toxic and nutritious in small amounts, ideally ground.

Frequently asked questions

Can dogs eat pumpkin seeds?

Yes. Plain, unsalted pumpkin seeds are non-toxic and nutritious in small amounts, ideally ground.

How much pumpkin seeds can dogs have?

Recommended: A few plain, unsalted. When in doubt, ask your vet.
