Can dogs eat tuna?

Dogs can eat tuna, but only in moderation.

A little plain tuna is fine, but it’s high in mercury and salt and shouldn’t be a regular food.

Frequently asked questions

Can dogs eat tuna?

In moderation. A little plain tuna is fine, but it’s high in mercury and salt and shouldn’t be a regular food.

How much tuna can dogs have?

Recommended: Small amounts, occasionally. When in doubt, ask your vet.
