Can dogs eat peanuts?

Dogs can eat peanuts, but only in moderation.

Plain unsalted peanuts are not toxic, but they are high in fat and a choking hazard, and salted ones are unhealthy.

Can dogs eat peanuts?

How much peanuts can dogs have?

Recommended: A few plain, unsalted. When in doubt, ask your vet.
