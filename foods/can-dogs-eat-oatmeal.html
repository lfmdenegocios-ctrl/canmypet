Can dogs eat oatmeal?

Yes — dogs can safely eat oatmeal.

Plain cooked oatmeal is a safe, fibre-rich treat and a gentle carbohydrate. Serve plain with no sugar or milk.

Frequently asked questions

Can dogs eat oatmeal?

Yes. Plain cooked oatmeal is a safe, fibre-rich treat and a gentle carbohydrate. Serve plain with no sugar or milk.

How much oatmeal can dogs have?

Recommended: Plain cooked, small amounts. When in doubt, ask your vet.
