Can dogs eat bacon?

Dogs can eat bacon, but only in moderation.

Bacon is very high in fat and salt, which can trigger stomach upset or pancreatitis, especially in small dogs.

Frequently asked questions

Can dogs eat bacon?

In moderation. Bacon is very high in fat and salt, which can trigger stomach upset or pancreatitis, especially in small dogs.

How much bacon can dogs have?

Recommended: A tiny piece, rarely. When in doubt, ask your vet.
