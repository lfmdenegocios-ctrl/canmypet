Can dogs eat edamame?

Yes — safe to feed to dogs.

Plain, shelled edamame is non-toxic and protein-rich in small amounts. Skip the salty seasoned kind.

Frequently asked questions

Can dogs eat edamame?

Yes. Plain, shelled edamame is non-toxic and protein-rich in small amounts. Skip the salty seasoned kind.

How much edamame can dogs have?

Recommended: Plain, shelled, small amounts. When in doubt, ask your vet.
