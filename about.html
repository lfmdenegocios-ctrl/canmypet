About us

Clear answers for worried pet parents

CanMyPet exists for one moment: when your dog or cat just ate something and you need to know — fast — whether it's safe.

Our mission

Every day, millions of people search "can my dog eat ___?" while standing in their kitchen, often a little panicked. The answers online are scattered, buried in long articles, or written to sell something. CanMyPet's mission is simple: give a clear, honest verdict in seconds — Yes, In moderation or Never — with the reason why and what to do next.

How we keep it accurate

Pet nutrition and toxicity is health information, and we treat it that way. Every food verdict on CanMyPet is written and reviewed by the CanMyPet Editorial Team against established, authoritative sources, and we show those sources on every page. When the science is uncertain, we say so and err on the side of caution.

  • We follow toxicity guidance from animal poison control and veterinary bodies.
  • We separate verdicts by species, because what's fine for a dog can be risky for a cat.
  • We review and update our database as guidance evolves.
  • We never diagnose. For anything urgent, we point you straight to your vet.

You can read exactly how we decide each verdict on our Sources & method page.

Who we are

CanMyPet is built by a small team of pet owners, writers and developers who were tired of frantically googling whether a dropped grape was a problem. We're not a vet clinic and we don't replace one — we're the fast, trustworthy first step before you call.
