How to choose the perfect pet name

  • Keep it short — 1–2 syllables are easiest for pets to learn and to call across a park.
  • Make it distinct from command words like "no," "sit" or "stay."
  • Say it out loud a few times — if it feels natural to shout happily, it's a keeper.
  • Test the reaction — try it for a day or two and see what your pet responds to.

Quick questions

What are the most popular dog names?

Classics like Luna, Bella, Charlie, Max, Cooper and Daisy top the lists year after year — try the "Classic" vibe.

Should the name match the breed?

It can be fun (a tiny "Tank," a fluffy "Marshmallow"), but it's totally optional — pick what makes you smile.

Can I use a human name for my pet?

Absolutely — Oscar, Lucy, Henry and other human names are some of the most popular pet names today.
