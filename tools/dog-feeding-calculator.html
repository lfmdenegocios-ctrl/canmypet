How the calculation works

The calculator uses the standard veterinary energy formula. First it finds your dog's Resting Energy Requirement (RER) — the calories needed at rest — then multiplies it by an activity factor to get the daily energy need:

  • RER = 70 × (body weight in kg)0.75
  • Daily calories = RER × activity factor
  • Food grams = daily calories ÷ food energy density

Because every dog and every food is different, treat the result as a starting point. Monitor your dog's body condition and adjust over a few weeks.
