A simple calendar for your pet's health

Keep every important date in one place — vaccines, deworming, flea & tick, medication, weigh-ins, vet visits and even heat cycles. Add a reminder and the list sorts itself by date, counts down the days, and flags anything overdue.

How it works

  1. Pick a type, a date and an optional note (like “Rabies” or “12.4 kg”).
  2. Hit Add — it slots into your list by date with a countdown.
  3. Anything overdue turns red so it's easy to catch up.
🔒 Private by design: everything is saved in your own browser (localStorage) — nothing is sent to us. Clearing your browser data will erase the list. For the exact vaccine timing, see our vaccine schedule and confirm with your vet.
