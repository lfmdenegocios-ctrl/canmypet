Which dog breed fits your lifestyle?

There's no single "best" breed — only the best fit for you. This quick quiz weighs your home, energy, grooming time, experience and household to suggest breeds that tend to thrive in a life like yours.

How it works

  1. Answer 5 quick questions about your day-to-day.
  2. We score 14 popular breeds against your answers.
  3. You get your top 3 matches with a fit score and the traits to expect.
🐾 A starting point, not a verdict: every dog is an individual. Meet the dog, talk to a reputable breeder or shelter, and please consider adoption — wonderful mixed-breeds are waiting too.
