The World Cup Snack Bracket — Pet-Safety Edition

🏆🐶⚽🐱🌭16 countries. 16 legendary game-day foods. One question at every watch party: "can my pet have a bite?" We checked each one, ingredient by ingredient.

The knockout rounds are here — and so are the watch-party spreads. Every country in the round of 16 brings a classic dish to the couch, and every dish gets the CanMyPet treatment: what's genuinely safe to share with your dog or cat, what needs care, and what should stay strictly on the human plate.

⚠️ Watch-party golden rule: if your pet grabs something dangerous, don't wait for symptoms. Call your vet or a poison line: ASPCA Animal Poison Control 888-426-4435 · Pet Poison Helpline 855-764-7661. Or use our 60-second emergency check.
